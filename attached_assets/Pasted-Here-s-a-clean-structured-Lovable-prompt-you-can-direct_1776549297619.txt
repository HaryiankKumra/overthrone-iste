## 🚀 Lovable Prompt: “Overthrone – Competitive Strategy Portal”

**Build a real-time web-based competition platform called “Overthrone”** with strategy, collaboration, and deception mechanics. The system should feel like a **kingdom warfare simulation + coding competition hybrid**.

---

### 🧠 Core Concept

* Duration: **3–4 hours**
* Divided into **epochs (rounds) of 15 minutes**
* Teams compete by solving tasks and strategically attacking others
* Victory is based on **combined Health Points (HP) + Attack Points (AP)**

---

### 🏰 Team System

* Teams can **register/login**
* Each team starts with:

  * **Health Points (HP): 10,000**
  * **Attack Points (AP): 0**
* Teams operate independently unless they form alliances

---

### 🃏 Card System (Core Mechanics)

Each team has access to **5 strategic cards**:

#### 1. Task Card

* Allows solving tasks (only 1 task at a time per team)
* Tasks can be:

  * Sudoku
  * Math puzzles
  * CTF challenges
  * Algorithmic problems

#### 2. Attack Card

* Used at the **end of each epoch**
* Teams spend AP to **reduce another team's HP**
* Cannot attack alliance members

#### 3. Alliance Card

* Two teams can form an alliance:

  * Combined HP + AP shown as one entity
  * Can solve **2 tasks simultaneously**
* Alliance is visible on leaderboard/map

#### 4. Backstab Card

* Used within an alliance
* Effects:

  * Steals task rewards earned by ally
  * Breaks alliance immediately

#### 5. Suspicion Card

* Used to accuse ally of backstab
* If accusation is:

  * ✅ Correct → Backstab team eliminated
  * ❌ Wrong → Accusing team disqualified

---

### ⚔️ Gameplay Mechanics

#### Task Solving

* Difficulty levels:

  * Easy → +150 AP
  * Medium → +300 AP
  * Hard → +500 AP
* Teams accumulate AP by solving tasks

#### Attack System

* At end of epoch:

  * Teams **must or can attack**
  * Choose:

    * Manual attack → select team
    * AI-based attack (optional advanced feature)

* Attack rules:

  * Cannot attack alliance partner
  * Targeted attacks cost extra AP (e.g., +50 AP)

---

### 🤖 AI/ML Feature (Optional but HIGH VALUE)

* Teams can choose:

  * Manual targeting OR
  * Upload/select a **basic ML model/strategy**
* AI suggests:

  * Best team to attack based on:

    * HP
    * AP
    * Alliance status
* System can simulate simple decision models if custom upload is too complex

---

### 🗺️ Visualization (VERY IMPORTANT)

Create a **real-time interactive map/dashboard**:

* Each team represented as a **kingdom/territory**
* Kingdom size = proportional to HP
* Alliances = merged territories
* Attacks = animated (e.g., arrows/fire effects)
* Theme: **medieval / war / kingdom**

---

### 📊 Leaderboard

* Live ranking based on:

  * Individual teams
